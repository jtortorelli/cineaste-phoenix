Stocky, official-looking actor who appeared regularly in Akira Kurosawa films as ronin, policemen, criminals, or heavyset authority figures. In particular he played one of the scheming samurai in <i>Sanjuro</i> (1962) and a corrupt businessman in <i>The Bad Sleep Well</i> (1960). He's best known to genre fans for playing generals fighting monsters in both <i>Monster Zero</i> (1965) and <i>Godzilla vs. Gigan</i> (1972).

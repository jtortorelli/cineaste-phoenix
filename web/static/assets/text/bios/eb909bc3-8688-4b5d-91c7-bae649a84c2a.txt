Tall, handsome, square-jawed actor, who is best known for playing Officer Ide in the original <i>Ultraman</i> TV series (1966). He joined Toho in 1961 through the New Face program and debuted in Kihachi Okamoto's <i>Blueprint of Murder</i> (1961). He appeared frequently in comedies such as the <i>Crazy Cats</i> series, and also appeared on the <i>Mighty Jack</i> TV series (1968, recut into a feature length film featured on <i>MST3K</i>). He has continued to appear in <i>Ultra</i> themed TV shows up to the present day.

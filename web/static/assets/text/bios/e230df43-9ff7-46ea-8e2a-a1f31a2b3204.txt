Talented actor in voice over and on screen. Mutsumi dubbed for Russ Tamblyn in the Japanese version of <i>War of the Gargantuas</i> (1966), as well as for David Janssen in the Japanese broadcast of <i>The Fugitive</i> TV series (1964-1967). He's best known to genre fans for appearing on screen as the leader of the alien apes in both <i>Mechagodzilla</i> films in the 70s.

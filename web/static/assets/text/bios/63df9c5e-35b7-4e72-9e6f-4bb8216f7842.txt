Cute actress known for her modest and wholesome image. Hoshi was recruited by Toho after winning in a beauty contest sponsored by the studio in 1958, and began appearing in films the next year.

She frequently co-starred with actor Yuzo Kayama, especially in Toho's <i>Young Guy</i> series of films.

Hoshi appeared in two <i>kaiju</i> films in 1964: <i>Mothra vs. Godzilla</i> and <i>Ghidorah, the Three-Headed Monster</i>. Her popularity among <i>kaiju</i> fans led to her return to the genre many years later in <i>Godzilla X Megaguirus</i> (2000).

Youthful actor who played mainly supporting roles in sci-fi films and TV. He had a prominent role on <i>Ultra Q</i> as Kenji Sahara's assistant. He shared fellow actor Yosuke Natsuki's love of cars. He apparently retired from acting shortly after the death of Eiji Tsuburaya, but he has reappeared in <i>Ultra</i>-themed productions as recently as 2008. One of his more prominent roles is as a scientist in <i>Son of Godzilla</i> (1967).

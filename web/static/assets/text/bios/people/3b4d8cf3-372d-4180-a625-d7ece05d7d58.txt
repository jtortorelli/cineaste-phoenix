Actor with boyish features who appeared in sci-fi just before it peaked in the late 60s. He also made appearances in the <i>Ultra</i> series (<i>Ultra Q</i>, <i>Ultraman</i>, etc.) He did not appear in any films following <i>Destroy All Monsters</i> (1968), and it's believed that he retired after Toho's contract system collapsed.

Maruyama's most prominent role was as one of the UN researchers in <i>Son of Godzilla</i> (1967).

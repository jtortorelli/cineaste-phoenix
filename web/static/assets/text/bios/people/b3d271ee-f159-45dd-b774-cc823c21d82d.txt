Prolific supporting actor appearing in small parts throughout the 60s in Toho films. Not much is known about his personal life. His more prominent roles are as a satellite engineer in the beginning of <i>Dogora, the Space Monster</i> (1964), an air traffic controller in <i>War of the Gargantuas</i> (1966), and as one of the first victims of the giant cuttlefish in <i>Space Amoeba</i> (1970).

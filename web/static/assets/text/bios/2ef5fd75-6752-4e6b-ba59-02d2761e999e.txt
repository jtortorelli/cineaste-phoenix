Tall, lean actor who often played henchmen. His sole "good guy" role appears to have been as the lost brother Yata in <i>Godzilla vs. the Sea Monster</i> (1966). He disappeared from the genre after <i>Destroy All Monsters</i> (1968) before making one last appearance as the goateed alien Tsuda in <i>Terror of Mechagodzilla</i> (1975).

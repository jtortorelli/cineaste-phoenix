Actress and wife of Toho producer Tomoyuki Tanaka. She joined Toho after finishing high school in 1944, and appeared in Akira Kurosawa's <i>One Wonderful Sunday</i> in 1947. She also appeared in a supporting role in several films by Mikio Naruse. Nakakita is best known to sci-fi fans as the grieving mother in the very beginning of <i>Godzilla vs. the Sea Monster</i> (1966) and as a mother desperately trying to reunite with her daughter towards the end of <i>The Last War</i> (1961).

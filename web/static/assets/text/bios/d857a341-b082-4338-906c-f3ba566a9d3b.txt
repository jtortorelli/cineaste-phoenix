American actor and dancer, best known for his appearances in musicals. His breakout role was as Gabriel, the youngest brother in <i>Seven Brides for Seven Brothers</i> (1954), and he's best known for appearing in <i>Tom Thumb</i> (1958) and in <i>West Side Story</i> (1961), as the leader of the Jets. In more recent years he starred as Dr. Jacoby in David Lynch's <i>Twin Peaks</i> TV series, and is expected to reprise the role in the 2017 revival.

Tamblyn appeared in <i>War of the Gargantuas</i> (1966), a Japanese-American co-production, to give film broader appeal in the States. He replaced actor Tab Hunter.
